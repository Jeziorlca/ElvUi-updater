<p>With this release we have changed how we handle UI scale in ElvUI.</p>
<p>As a result we no longer set, or rely on, the CVar for UI scale. Because of this your UI scale has to be set again when you first log in with this new version.</p>
<p>We have done our best to make it a graceful transition, and as such you will be presented with a popup on your first log-in, where you can see what your old CVar value was, and choose to use this value. <p>From now on you can choose your UI scale within the ElvUI config, or press the "Auto Scale" button to use the value that was previously considered most optimal for your resolution.</p>
